Frequently asked questions

Before you download.

Do I need to enter an email address?

No. The page provides a direct download of the Excel workbook.

Can I use the workbook for more than one year?

Yes. Save a fresh copy for each year or duplicate the workbook before replacing your previous figures.

Does the workbook calculate totals for me?

Yes. The annual spending planner includes formulas for category totals, annual amounts, total expenses, and cash short-or-extra calculations.

Is this financial advice?

No. The workbook is an educational budgeting resource. Suggested percentage ranges are general guidelines and may not fit every household or location.
